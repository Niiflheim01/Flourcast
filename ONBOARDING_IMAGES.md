# Onboarding Images Setup Guide

## Required Images

You need to add 4 images to the following location:
**`project/assets/images/`**

### Image Names and Pages:

1. **`onboarding-1.png`** - Opening/Loading Screen
   - The Flourcast logo screen
   - Should contain the bread/bakery logo with "FLOURCAST" text
   - Background: Light beige/cream (#E8DCC8 or similar)

2. **`onboarding-2.png`** - Starting Page (Sales)
   - "Stay on top of every sale" page
   - Illustration of person with tablet/clipboard showing sales graphs
   - Shows real-time sales tracking concept

3. **`onboarding-3.png`** - Starting Page (Inventory)
   - "Keep track of your ingredients and products easily" page  
   - Illustration of person with boxes/packages and search icon
   - Shows inventory management concept

4. **`onboarding-4.png`** - Starting Page (Forecasting)
   - "Bake the right amount, every time" page
   - Illustration of baker with tablet showing production planning
   - Shows demand prediction concept

## Instructions:

1. Save each screenshot/image from your design
2. Rename them according to the names above
3. Place them in: `project/assets/images/`
4. Images should be PNG format
5. Recommended size: 800x800 pixels or similar square/portrait ratio
